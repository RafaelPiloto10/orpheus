Five little ducks  
Went out one day  
Over the hill and far away  
Mother duck said  
"Quack, quack, quack, quack."  
But only four little ducks came back.

Went out one day  
Over the hill and far away  
Mother duck said  
"Quack, quack, quack, quack."  
But none of the five little ducks came back.  
  
Sad mother duck  
Went out one day  
Over the hill and far away  
The sad mother duck said  
"Quack, quack, quack."  
And all of the five little ducks came back.
